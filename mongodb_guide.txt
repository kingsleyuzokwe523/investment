MONGODB ATLAS SETUP GUIDE
=========================

1. LOGIN: https://cloud.mongodb.com
   - Use your MongoDB credentials

2. NAVIGATE to IP Whitelist:
   - Left Sidebar: Click "Security"
   - Then click: "Database & Network Access"

3. CHECK Current IPs:
   - Look at the table
   - You need either:
     * 0.0.0.0/0 (Allow from anywhere)
     * OR your specific IP address

4. ADD IP if missing:
   - Click GREEN "Add IP Address" button
   - Click "Allow Access from Anywhere"
   - Click "Confirm"

5. WAIT 3 minutes

6. TEST Connection:
   python backend/test_mongo_final.py

Common Issues:
- Forgot to click "Confirm"
- Didn't wait long enough
- Wrong MongoDB account
